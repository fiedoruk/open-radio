# ADR-006 — Founder attribution

**Status:** ACCEPTED
**Date:** 2026-07-13

## Decision

The founder and original author is:

> Tomasz Fiedoruk — https://fiedoruk.pl

This attribution appears in `AUTHORS.md`, `CITATION.cff`, public repository
metadata and the device `About / O projekcie` screen. Copyright notices use:

> Copyright 2026 Tomasz Fiedoruk

The final project name and original project logo remain separate brand assets
and are not granted by the source-code license.

## Contribution attribution

Contributors retain authorship of their changes through Git, DCO sign-off and
the contributor record. Founder attribution must not be used to imply endorsement
of a fork.
