The project name **Open Radio** and the final released mark (as used on
`fiedoruk.pl/open-radio` and in official release artifacts) are brand assets
of Tomasz Fiedoruk, reserved and separate from the source-code, documentation
and station-data licenses. The source license does not grant permission to
imply that Tomasz Fiedoruk or `fiedoruk.pl` endorses a fork, and downstream
builds must not present themselves as official releases.

Exact map: everything under `brand/` in this repository is original concept
artwork licensed CC BY-SA 4.0 like the rest of the project artwork; the
reserved final mark ships only with official releases and the project site.
Official builds are identified by the name, the mark and the official
download location (`fiedoruk.pl/os/`) together.

M5Stack and Core2 are names or marks of their respective owners. The current
default nine names RMF FM, Radio ZET, TOK FM, Antyradio, Radio ESKA,
RMF MAXX, Radio Plus, Złote Przeboje and Meloradio, and the searchable
directory names further public stations. Historical design documents also
mention VOX FM, Radio Pogoda and ESKA Impreska. All broadcaster and
station names or marks remain the property of their respective owners and are
used only to identify public stations or dated engineering scope.

This project is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by or
sponsored by M5Stack or any broadcaster.

The project supports original per-station themes and private local artwork packs.
Do not submit official station logos, jingles, advertising assets or broadcaster
brand-book derivatives without documented permission. Third-party assets must be
marked as excluded from the project license.
